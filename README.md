## University Subject Competition Platform

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/4383b039c51343a9a3d40e0a4982811e/ghb20250918) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (any of the listed software can be used)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Functional Description
The system is divided into (roles): regular users, teachers, administrators.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Competition Information
5. Competition News
6. Personal Center

**Teacher Functions:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
4. Question Type Management
5. Competition Question Bank Management
6. Competition Type Management
7. Competition Information Management
8. Registration Application Management
9. Competition Scoring Management
10. Participant List Management
11. Advancement List Management
12. Award List Management
13. Competition Summary Management
14. Reimbursement List Management
15. Grade Appeal Management
16. Participation Information Management
17. Past Performance Management
18. Award Status Management



**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
3. Teacher Management
4. Student Management
5. Team Leader Teacher Management
6. Competition Type Management
7. Competition Information Management
8. College Management
9. Major Management
10. Award Status Management (Carousel Management, Competition News)
